Intro:

Hello! This is for DKU's 2024 HACKATHON

Our application strives to act as a middleman between insurance companies and consumers, ensuring that both parties mutually benefit from our intervention.

First, we aim to collaborate with major insurance firms to ensure our blockchain tokens will be accepted.

Then, our app will launch to their consumers. They will be able to log in with the same credentials as their purchased plan.

The app will survey each user and collect responses, then using AI technology, generate a list of tasks for them to complete.

These tasks will be productive, with the goal of reducing their risk in whatever insurance plan they are enrolled in.

For instance, our main focus was home insurance, so one task that could be generated is checking your fire alarms or sprinkler systems.

Other tasks can include reinforcing your home or installing a security camera to guard against thefts.

Once these tasks are completed, an individual will send proof in the form of an invoice or other photo evidence.

This will be verified by IoF technology, analyzed by us, and communicated to the insurance company.

As a reward, the task-completer will get a predetermined non-fungible token (worth more value according to task difficulty).

These tokens can be directly exchanged for discounts in insurance as negotiated with the insurance company.

Exchanged NFTs will be returned to us, and we will only mint a limited number of each discount token, increasing their value for consumers in times of disaster while allowing consumers to pay less during safe times.

This optimizes consumer spending as their plan price changes based on individual actions. The insurance companies also benefit as we will share consumer data, willingly input, to them.
